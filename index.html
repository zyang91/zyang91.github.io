My Research Interests

As a growing urban planer, I am particular drawn to the ways in which Geographic Information Systems (GIS) can help us better understand and plan our cities. Through my academic courses, I have gained a solid understanding of what GIS tools are and how these technologies can be used to collect and analyze spatial data, such as population demographics, land use patterns, and transportation networks. I am interested in exploring how GIS can be used to improve the urban planning process and people's engagement, especially in the areas of transportation planning and sustainable development. Ultimately, I believe GIS has the potential to revolutionize the way we plan and way of designing cities, and I'm excited to be part of this dynamic field.
